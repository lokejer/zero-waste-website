Hi! I'm Jerome.

I'm passionate about eco-friendly living and believe that sustainability starts with small, meaningful changes. My journey into zero-waste living began a few years ago, and ever since, I’ve embraced habits that help reduce my footprint—both at home and in the community.

From composting food scraps to swapping out single-use plastics for reusable essentials, I've learned that mindful choices matter. One of my favourite green hacks is crafting my own all-purpose cleaner with vinegar, citrus peels, and essential oils—it's effective, fragrant, and helps cut back on packaging waste.

I also love upcycling old clothes and giving new life to items that would otherwise be discarded. It’s a creative challenge—and a great reminder that sustainability can be stylish and fun too!

To me, living green isn’t about being perfect—it’s about being intentional. I’m always learning, experimenting, and sharing tips to make eco-living more accessible, rewarding, and enjoyable. Thanks for being part of the journey!
