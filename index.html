Zero Waste Principles

Refuse

» Say no to things you don't truly need «

Politely decline freebies, junk mail, or single-use plastic bags. This stops waste before it even starts.

Reduce

» Buy and use less to minimise waste «

Choose products with less packaging, or only shop when necessary. This limits what enters your home.

Reuse

» Find new purposes for old/unwanted things «

Use glass jars for storage, repair clothes, or repurpose containers instead of throwing them away.

Recycle

» Take the extra step - Sort your trash! «

Put clean recyclables like paper, metal, and certain plastics into Singapore's blue recycling bins.

Composting

» Return nutrients to the Earth. «

Compost fruit and vegetable scraps to enrich soil instead of sending them to the landfill.
